How to Donate

Those wishing to donate to the campaign can send their contributions to the National Drought Mitigation Appeal Fund under KCB Bank, Riverside branch through account number 1305486137. Donations can also be done through M-PESA, with PayBill will also be available to Airtel Money and Telkom’s T-Kash subscribers.
